Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind

By Yuval Noah Harari

Yuval Noah Harari's 'Sapiens' is a breathtaking journey through the history of humankind. It masterfully connects history, biology, and economics to explain how Homo sapiens came to dominate the planet. This thought-provoking book challenges your perspectives on society, happiness, and the very essence of being human.
