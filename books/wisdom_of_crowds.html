The Wisdom of Crowds

By James Surowiecki

This book makes a compelling case that large groups of diverse, independent people are often smarter than a few experts. It explores how collective intelligence shapes business, economies, and societies. A fascinating read that explains why prediction markets work, why diversity matters, and how to tap into collective wisdom.
