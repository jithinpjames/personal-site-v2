Steve Jobs

I came to know about him after reading his autobiography and was inspired by how a person from a normal background could create a lasting impact on world history. The butterfly effect of his typography class impacting how we all see fonts in our computer applications is a powerful lesson.

From him, I'm learning to adapt simple designs, do honest work, and obsess over quality. His journey is a testament to the power of combining artistry with technology and maintaining an unwavering focus on the user experience.
