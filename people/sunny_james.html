My Father, Sunny James

Sunny James, basketball coach at Cherupushpam Higher Secondary School in rural Chandanakampara, Kannur, built a remarkable talent pipeline despite limited resources, guiding students to state, national, and international success.

Notable alumni include Ivy Cherian, former Indian women’s team captain and dominant center; Bobit Mathew, long-time Kerala captain and India junior representative who earned multiple state honors; Thankachan MC, who went on to coach the Indian Army and serve as assistant coach of India’s senior team; and Johnson Xavier, who won a bronze medal in rowing at the 1999 Asian Games.

His legacy shows how hard work, dedication, and a deep commitment to society can create extraordinary outcomes, even with the most limited resources.
