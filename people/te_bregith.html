My Mother, T.E. Bregith A

T.E. Bregith A, a Sanskrit teacher by profession, has been the quiet yet unwavering pillar of my life. While my father was often occupied with work, she carried the responsibility of supporting our family with strength and grace. It was she who introduced me to the world of books at a very young age, nurturing my curiosity and love for learning long before I understood its value.

More importantly, she shaped the foundations of my character—teaching me to be truthful, honest, compassionate, and hardworking. Much of who I am today is rooted in her discipline, values, and selfless commitment.
