Quiet Teachers

A tribute to the individuals whose work, wisdom, and character have profoundly shaped my personal and professional life.

Sunny James

"His legacy shows how hard work, dedication, and a deep commitment to society can create extraordinary outcomes..."

A basketball coach who built a remarkable talent pipeline from a rural school, guiding students to national and international success.

T.E. Bregith A

"Much of who I am today is rooted in her discipline, values, and selfless commitment."

A Sanskrit teacher and the quiet, unwavering pillar of my life who nurtured my curiosity and shaped my character.

Ruchir Sharma

"The old rule of forecasting was to make as many forecasts as possible and publicise the ones you got right. The new rule is to forecast so far in the future, no one will know you got it wrong."

Investor and author known for his insights on emerging markets and global economic trends.

Prashant Kishor

"One must always believe in the wisdom of the crowd."

A political strategist renowned for his work on election campaigns and public policy.

Nikhil Kamath

"Nothing hurts a man more than feeling like you’re growing older without making meaningful progress in your life."

Co-founder of Zerodha, who has redefined the landscape of retail investing in India.

Steve Jobs

"You can’t connect the dots looking forward; you can only connect them looking backward. So you have to trust that the dots will somehow connect in your future."

A visionary who created a lasting impact on world history through design, innovation, and relentless perfectionism.
